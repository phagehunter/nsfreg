About this site

An unofficial, community-built guide to help NSF-affiliated researchers and administrators understand and prepare for NSF's forthcoming prohibition on collaborations with restricted entities. Not affiliated with, or endorsed by, the U.S. National Science Foundation or any federal agency. Nothing here is legal advice.
